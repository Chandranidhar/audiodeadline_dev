Our Host Flii Stylz

Our phenomenal President and host of our show, AudioDeadline, is here to drive the music revolution with you in the front seat. Flii is an immensely talented industry titan, performing artist, choreographer, and music executive who has worked in the music industry for many years gaining experience and building meaningful relationships.

Flii was only four years old when he began his career as a dancer in his hometown of Oakland, California. When he was 5, he entered his first dance competition against kids that were twice and three times his age and came in third place. Since then he has worked with musical legends like Michael Jackson, Chris Brown, Jennifer Lopez, User, and so many more! Flii has won multiple awards that include MTV Award Winner for Best Choreography and the American Choreography Award for Outstanding Achievement in Choreography in Music Video. He didn’t stop there, he has also joined many stars on their international tours and has even worked on movie sets and commercials.

NETWORK APPEARANCES

Flii’s choreography and dance management can be discovered in over 300 well-known and successful music videos. He has been a massive contributor to over 100 live performance sets. These include major mainstream artists such as Chris Brown, Usher, David Guetta, Nicki Minaj, Tyrese, Gloria Estefan, J Lo, Rick Ross, Ludacris, Kendrick Lamar, the list goes on and on.

As a performing artist, Flii has produced collaborations with Will.I.Am and the multi-platinum super-group, The Black Eyed Peas, soul singer Martin Luther, Grammy-nominated rapper Planet Asia and others. He performed live as a rap artist on the MTV Music Video Awards. He appeared as a major actor and lyrical voice of the Krump movement in David LaChapelle’s breakthrough documentary, “Rize”.

Flii also has major exposure in many films, related to music and choreography. He co-wrote and starred in Usher’s “Rhythm City Vol.1: Caught Up”, contributed as an artist in Stomp The Yard, Scary Movie, and several more. He appeared many times on major television networks including Turner Digital, The CW, BET, HBO, Showtime and has been a special guest on Oprah, Jay Leno, and more.
