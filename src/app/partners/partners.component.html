Beto Paredes

Family of Companies

The owner of the company Beto Paredes’ background is extremely diverse and ranges from core software engineering, entrepreneur training, presenting, international sales, massive sales organizational management, business management, award-winning branding, and web development. Starting in the year 2002, Beto Paredes has worked as a professional in the internet space for 16+ years. Many of his projects have seen revenues into the millions and he continues to launch successful platforms to this day. Among being a successful businessman, Beto has had a passion for music since his younger years. His passion flourished when he was 14 years old and bought his very first guitar. He was in 13 different bands in total, between the ages of 14 and 27 years old. Everything that he focused on and cared about was music. He has written well over 50 songs. Seventh Sight, one of his many bands, had a regular show in Salt Lake City, UT that was always packed. Beto Paredes knows the struggle that independent talents in the entertainment industry face day in and day out. He partnered up with AudioDeadline becoming the CEO because he believes everybody should have the opportunity to showcase their talent.

Alfa Blvck

Every ​once​ and awhile, you come across an artist that immediately captivates your attention. Alfa Blvck​ (Rahim Blocker) age 29, was born and raised in Harlem. In New York, Alfa is at the forefront of the underground rap scene. His unique name comes from his ​love for comic superheroes.​ His uncanny ability to ​black out​ on every track gave rise to a name that symbolized that resolve. What separates Alfa from the rest is his almost endless well of creativity when it comes to releasing content. He has ​mastered​ the art of making his voice sound ​exactly​ how he wants, and therefore ​engineers and masters all of his songs.​ He also did the video editing for his recent music video release "FYTB." Not only is he a ​creative genius,​ he is also a ​master wordsmith.​ Songs like "The Gauntlet" easily injects him into the ranks of rap superstars like ​Kendrick Lamar and J. Cole.​ Alfa Blvck is an independent artist armed with all the required tools to slam the rap game on its head and solidify New York as its top contender.“I measure a man by the extent of his sacrifices...”​ the response when asked how does he decide who to work with or not. ​Packing crowds​ at Highline Ballroom and hosting ​epic release parties​ was a dream to Alfa. He graduated high school and went to Penn State, completing two years before deciding to drop out and ​host his own events at the university.​ Returning home was the beginning of the ​struggle​ when he made the decision to pursue his music career full time. He dealt with on and off ​homelessness​ and wasn’t able to maintain a ​steady love life. His commitment to his dreams lead him down a hard path, and you can hear the results of doing things his way every time he ​speaks​ or ​releases music.
