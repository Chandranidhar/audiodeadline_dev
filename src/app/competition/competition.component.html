Competition

Fulfill your dreams of becoming a musician by entering our quarterly competitions! They will run for 45 days, 4 times a year - so you have more chances to get onto The AudioDeadline Experience where you will have only eight hours to create an original song with other contest winners. Join our social media network, ArtistXP.com, to make your profile and start uploading your media. Next, head over to the competition page and enter the one you would like to participate in so that users can immediately start voting for you. Users can vote up to five times a day.

We offer several competitions that will change every quarter.
The different competitions are DJ, Guitarist, Drummer, Singer/Vocalist, Backup singer,
Producer, Dancer, and Model. With The AudioDeadline Experience debut,
the first competitions will be for vocalist, rapper, producer, dancer (2 winners), and models.

Competitions start on July 1st, 2019.

If you’re the lucky winner, you are immediately treated like a star! You are flown out to LA with paid room and board. From here you receive an exclusive interview and dinner with the AudioDeadline Executive team and our President and host, the amazing Flii Stylz, who is an industry titan and has worked with various music legends from Michael Jackson to Jennifer Lopez. The excitement doesn’t end there, you will be working in a real live sound studio in front of multiple cameras, getting that industry exposure you’ve been waiting for.
